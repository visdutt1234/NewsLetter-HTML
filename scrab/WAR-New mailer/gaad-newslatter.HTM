Maruti launches Baleno automatic in new trim-level
Maruti launched the Baleno in India last year. The car is selling like hot cakes and is being exported to over 100 countries. The Baleno has become quite popular in the Indian market and has been attracting a lot of buyers. Following the success of the car, Maruti has launched a new top-end variant of the automatic version of the Baleno. The new Zeta variant that was earlier available with the manual transmission is now available with the CVT option and sits above the earlier available mid-trim Delta.
